Classic Italian Pasta

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1 person
  • Difficulty: Easy
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 100g
Olive Oil 2 tbsp
Garlic 2 cloves

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and saut√© sliced garlic until golden.
  3. Toss the pasta in the garlic oil and serve with black pepper.

Energy Fruit Smoothie

  • Time: 5 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
  • Difficulty: Very Easy
Ingredients Quantity
Banana 1 large
Frozen Berries 1/2 cup
Yogurt 200ml

Preparation:

First, peel the banana and break it into pieces. Put all ingredients into a blender. Blend on high speed for 45 seconds until the mixture is smooth and creamy. Pour into chilled glasses and enjoy immediately!
